About Us

At GoodDeed, we are committed to make job opportunities more available and accessible for everyone.

Founded with the goal of bridging the gap between volunteers and prospective hosts, GoodDeed simplifies the volunteer experience by matching volunteers with opportunities based on their interests and availability. For hosts, we offer tools to post opportunities and manage volunteers, reducing costs.

By creating a connected community of helpers and changemakers, our mission is to drive greater participation in volunteerism, increase the impact of service, and help nonprofits maximize their positive contributions to society.

Join us in building a volunteer experience where doing good deeds is easier than ever!
